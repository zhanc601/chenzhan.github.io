About

I am a PhD student in Social Policy at the University of Birmingham, supervised by Dr. Ellie Suh and Dr. Louise Overton. My research focuses on housing, inequality, and intergenerational support in China.

My work combines quantitative and qualitative approaches to study how housing policies shape family strategies and welfare outcomes.
